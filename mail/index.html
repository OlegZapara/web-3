Why you need a cat

Cats make wonderful companions and can bring a lot of joy to your life. They are independent yet affectionate, and their playful nature can keep you entertained. Owning a cat can also provide stress relief, as studies have shown that petting a cat can lower blood pressure and reduce anxiety. Additionally, cats are low-maintenance pets, making them a great choice for people with busy lifestyles. Their natural hunting instincts can also help keep your home free of pests.
